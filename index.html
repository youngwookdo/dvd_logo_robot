Saver from Screen

Saver from Screen is a mobile robot that draws attention away from digital displays and back into physical space. Inspired by the bouncing DVD logo, it moves across the floor as if reflecting off invisible screen edges, creating a familiar yet uncanny presence. The project reimagines the idea of a screensaver: instead of protecting a screen, it offers relief from one. In a world filled with phones, laptops, and public displays, the robot acts as a small distraction with a different purpose—to give the eyes and mind a brief rest, and to invite people to look away.
